Training

Our training stream takes a holistic approach to the personal and professional development of the Fellows.

Curriculum is based on the continuous feedback of both our students and corporate partners. It has been developed specifically with the experiences and capacities of our students, applied directly to the needs of our clients. Classes are highly interactive and delivered in a workshop format. The coursework is dynamic, much of which is taught by corporate partners.

Topics include:

  • Human Centered Design: An Introduction
  • Applying research & critical thinking to seeing your “self” in the business world
  • Presentation & Public Speaking
  • Building Social Capital & Professional Development
  • Soft Skills: Teamwork, planning and prioritizing, customer service, account management, communication and conflict management, problem-solving
  • Breaking Through Imposter Syndrome: Assertiveness, self-confidence, body language, mindfulness, understanding workplace culture
  • An Introduction to Tools for Analytics
  • Entrepreneurship and the Business of You
  • The Power of Giving Back: Community workshop
