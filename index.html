Working with nontraditional university students, Employtech provides internship placements for companies as a way to provide employers with access to untapped talent.

Our students are the leaders of tomorrow...equipped to innovate, and ready to dig in!

In 2015, the World Economic Forum published a report stating that social and emotional skills (Competencies and Character Qualities) are increasingly critical for employees to meaningfully contribute to workplaces of the 21st century.

Competencies include:

  • Creativity
  • Critical Thinking / Problem Solving
  • Collaboration
  • Communication

Character Qualities are:

  • Persistence / Grit
  • Leadership
  • Curiosity
  • Initiative
  • Social / Cultural Awareness

Our students are chosen to participate as Employtech fellows based on their aptitude for the Character Qualities, and the curriculum and learning experiences incorporate the Competencies skills throughout the process.
